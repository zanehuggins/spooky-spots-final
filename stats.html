WHEN WERE THESE PLACES IN OPERATION?

Most of these places have been out of operation for several decades. Just because they have been abandoned for a long time doesn't mean they don't have neighbors nearby, so you have to be careful not to draw any attention to yourself. Factories often have businesses that are still open operating all around them. Others, are abandoned in the woods and haven't been in use since the 60s.

HOW DO I PLAN TO GO EXPLORING?

Planning for an urban exploration trip requires research and preparation. How many structure there are to explore should be taken into consideration as it will determine how much time you will spend at the location. You will also need to know how secluded your destination will be, as this will likely impact the amount of people in your group, what kind of vehicle you take, and what supplies are required. Planning at least a week out is ideal, as people often change their plans and you may need to travel in a large group.
